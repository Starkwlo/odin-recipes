Homemade Lasagna


Lasagna is a hearty layered dish, perfect for the body and soul!

  • 1/2 pound ground pork
  • 1/2 pound lead ground beef
  • 1/2 cup minced onion
  • 1 cal crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons chopped parsley, divided
  • 1 clove garlic, crushed
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons dried basil
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/8 teaspoon white sugar
  • 1 (16 oz) package lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound small-curd cottage cheese
  • 3/4 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 3 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 (16 ounce) package shredded mozzarella cheese
  1. Gather Ingredients
  2. Combine pork and ground beef in a large skillet over medium-high heat; cook and stir until browned
  3. Add onion and cook until translucent, about 5 minutes
  4. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, 1 tbl spoon fresh parsley, garlic, basil, salt, oregano, and sugar. Reduce heat to medium-low and simmer, stirring occasionally, for 30 minutes.
  5. While the sauce is simmering, bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook lasagna noodles in the boiling water, stirring occasionally, until tender yet firm to the bite, 8 to 10 minutes. Drain and set aside. While the noodles are cooking, preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 C)
  6. Mix cottage cheese, parmesan cheese, eggs, remaining 1 tblspoon fresh parsley, salt and peppper in a large bowl until combined.
  7. Assemble lasagna; spread a spoon or two of sauce over bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish just to coat it. Place two layers of noodles over the sauce to cover.
  8. Layer with 1/2 of the cheese mixture, 1/2 of the remainging sauce, and 1/2 of the mozzarella cheese. Repeat layers once more using the ramainging noodles, cheese mixture, sauce, and mozzarella. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil.
  9. Bake in the preheated oven for 30 to 40 minutes. Remove the foil and bake until cheese is golden brown, 5 to 10 more minutes.
  10. Remove from the oven and let stand for 10 minutes before cutting and serving.
